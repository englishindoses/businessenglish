Activity 1/7

Grammar Review

Here is a summary of the four grammar points from Lessons 4-7. Review the form and examples, then discuss any questions with your teacher.

📘 Lesson 4: Modal Verbs for Politeness

Form: could / would / might + base verb

  • Could you send me the report?
  • Would you mind forwarding this to the team?
  • Would it be possible to reschedule the meeting?

📗 Lesson 5: Future Forms for Arrangements

Forms: present continuous (confirmed), going to (planned), will (spontaneous).

  • I'm meeting the client at 3pm tomorrow.
  • I'm going to prepare some updated figures.
  • Great! I'll send you a Teams invite.

📙 Lesson 6: Second Conditional for Suggestions

Form: If + past simple, ... would/could/might + infinitive.

  • If we offered flexible working hours, we would attract more candidates.
  • If we increased the training budget, we could improve staff retention.
  • What if we hired more staff? Would that help?

📕 Lesson 7: Reported Speech for Summarising

Form: said/mentioned/explained (that) + tense backshift.

  • She said that she thought they should delay the hiring process.
  • He mentioned that they had received over fifty applications.
  • They explained that they couldn't increase the starting salary.

Activity 6/7

Meeting Gap Fill

Read this meeting dialogue and choose the correct expression for each gap.

👥

The Context

A project team is meeting to discuss whether they can meet their product launch deadline. The manager, Ana and Carlos are sharing ideas.

1 Manager: "Thanks for joining, everyone. Ana, what are your thoughts on the timeline?"

Ana: "Sure. , we could use more time for the testing phase."

2 Manager responds to Ana:

Manager: " . Carlos, what do you think?"

3 Carlos shares his view:

Carlos: " we could manage if we had more support."

4 Ana agrees and makes a suggestion:

Ana: "You make a good point. Would that help with the workload?"

5 Manager responds with a concern:

Manager: "That's worth considering, though I'd need to check on that before we make a decision."

6 Carlos adds another idea:

Carlos: "And we could save another few days too."
